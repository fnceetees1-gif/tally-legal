Frequently asked questions

How do I add a habit?

Tap + New habit on the home screen, give it a name, pick a color, and start checking in each day.

What is Tally Pro?

The free version lets you track up to three habits. Tally Pro is a one-time purchase that unlocks unlimited habits, all colors and icons, full streak history, and home-screen widgets. There is no subscription — you pay once and it’s yours forever.

I bought Tally Pro on another device. How do I restore it?

Open the Pro screen and tap Restore Purchases. As long as you’re signed in with the same Apple ID, your purchase will be restored at no extra charge.

Where is my data stored?

All of your habits and streaks are stored privately on your device. Tally does not collect or upload any of your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

I lost my data after deleting the app.

Because your data is stored only on your device for privacy, uninstalling the app removes it. We recommend keeping the app installed to preserve your streaks.
